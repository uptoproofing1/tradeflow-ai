Privacy Policy

Effective date: 31 May 2026 · Last updated: 31 May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Up Top Technologies ("QuotaFlo", "we", "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the QuotaFlo AI application and website (the "App").

Contact: support.quotaflo@gmail.com
Operator / data controller: Up Top Technologies, Greymouth, West Coast, New Zealand

1. Summary (plain English)

  • QuotaFlo is a tool for tradespeople to create quotes and invoices, scan supplier invoices, generate safety documents, track mileage, and use an AI assistant.
  • Most of your business data lives on your device (in your browser's local storage): your jobs, quotes, invoices, materials, trips, client details, and your business profile. We do not have a central copy of this data.
  • Some features send data to our server and to third-party AI providers to work — for example, generating a quote, scanning a photo of an invoice, or producing a safety pack. Those flows are described in Section 3.
  • We use third parties for sign-in, payment, and AI processing. They are listed in Section 5.
  • Honest security note: data stored on your device is obfuscated, not strongly encrypted, unless you create an encrypted backup (which is). See Section 7.

2. Who this applies to

The App is intended for business users (sole traders and tradespeople). You may enter information about your clients (names, addresses, phone numbers, job details, prices). You are responsible for having a lawful basis to enter that information and for telling your clients how their information is used. We process it on your behalf.

3. What we collect and why

3.1 Information stored on your device only

The following is stored in your browser's local storage on your device and is not sent to our servers by the App in normal use:

  • Your business profile: your name, company name, business location, phone number, and (optionally) a company logo.
  • Your business records: jobs, quotes, invoices, materials, mileage trips, and client details you enter (which may include client names, addresses, phone numbers, emails, job descriptions, and prices/GST).
  • App settings (e.g. GST rate, theme) and a local lock PIN.

This data stays on your device unless you choose to (a) use a feature that sends specific data to a third party (Section 3.2), (b) create a backup (Section 7), or (c) share a quote for approval (Section 3.3).

3.2 Information processed by AI features

When you use an AI feature, the relevant input is sent over an encrypted (HTTPS) connection to our server, which forwards it to a third-party AI provider, and the result is returned to you:

Voice note: Speech-to-text is performed by your browser's built-in speech recognition (the Web Speech API). In some browsers (e.g. Google Chrome) the audio of your voice is sent by the browser to the browser vendor (e.g. Google) for transcription. The App itself does not record, store, or transmit your voice audio to our servers — we only ever receive the resulting text. This processing is governed by your browser vendor's privacy policy.

3.3 Quote approval

If you send a client a quote-approval link, the quote and an approval status are stored in our database (Supabase) so the client can approve it from their device. This may include the client's name and the quote contents.

3.4 Account and authentication

If you create an account, we (via Supabase) collect your email address and a securely hashed password to sign you in.

3.5 Payments

Subscriptions are processed by Stripe via a hosted checkout page on our website. We do not receive or store your full card details; Stripe handles payment information under its own privacy policy. We receive a subscription status.

3.6 Location

If you enable weather or mileage tracking, the App uses your device location to:

  • fetch local weather (sent to Open-Meteo), and
  • look up your approximate locality name (sent to BigDataCloud), and
  • record trip distances (stored on your device as mileage records).

You can decline the location permission; those features then do not work, but the rest of the App does.

3.7 Camera and microphone

  • Camera is used to photograph supplier invoices and job sites for the scan/safety features (Section 3.2).
  • Microphone is used for voice input (Section 3.2).

Both are only accessed when you actively use those features.

3.8 Technical/diagnostic data

We may process limited technical data necessary to operate the service (e.g. request metadata to our API). As of the effective date, the App ships with no third-party analytics or crash-reporting SDK. If we add one in future, we will update this policy and our Google Play Data Safety form.

4. How we use information

  • To provide the App's features (quotes, invoices, scanning, safety packs, mileage, AI assistant, read-aloud).
  • To authenticate you and manage your subscription.
  • To operate, maintain, secure, and improve the service.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your business data or your clients' data to train our own models. AI providers process it to return a result; their use is governed by their terms (Section 5).

6. Retention

  • On-device data stays until you delete it, clear your browser storage, or uninstall.
  • Account data (email) is retained while your account is active. If you delete your account, your account record and quote-approval records are deleted within 30 days.
  • Data sent to AI providers is retained according to their policies; we do not keep a separate copy beyond what is needed to return your result.

7. Security — and an honest limitation

  • Data sent between the App, our server, and providers is protected in transit with HTTPS/TLS.
  • A local PIN can lock the App on your device.
  • Important honesty note: the routine on-device storage uses lightweight obfuscation, not strong encryption, so it should not be relied on as protection if your unlocked device is accessed by someone else. Encrypted backups that you create with a passphrase are strongly encrypted (AES-GCM with PBKDF2-SHA-256 key derivation). Keep your backup passphrase safe — we cannot recover it.
  • No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

8. Your choices and rights

  • Access / export / delete: Most of your data is on your device — you can view and delete it in the App, and export an encrypted backup. To delete your account and any server-side records (email, quote approvals), use Settings → Delete Account in the App, visit our account deletion page, or email support.quotaflo@gmail.com.
  • Permissions: You can grant or revoke camera, microphone, and location permissions in your device/browser settings.
  • Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal information (e.g. under the NZ Privacy Act 2020, GDPR, or Australian Privacy Act). Contact us to exercise them.

9. Children

The App is for business use and is not intended for anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. International transfers

Our providers may process data in other countries (e.g. the United States). Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards for those transfers.
